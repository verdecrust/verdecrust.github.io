Our Story

Founded in 2023, Verde Crust brings you authentic vegetarian pizzas made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Our passion for perfect crusts and innovative veggie toppings has made us a favorite in the community.

Our Philosophy

We believe pizza should be both delicious and wholesome. That's why we:

  • Use 100% vegetarian ingredients
  • Source organic produce whenever possible
  • Make our dough fresh daily
  • Craft each pizza with care
